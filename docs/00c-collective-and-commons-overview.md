# v0.3 Overview — Collective Intelligence, the Commons, Buildability & Verification

> *Look at one jewel and you see all the others reflected in it. Doc 00 cut the first eight facets and arranged the net so each reflects the rules of the whole. Doc 00b grew the net outward — more working jewels, a bridge to other nets, a gated way to make more. This third front door does not grow the net outward again; it **instruments the reflections themselves.** It asks the question the first two layers deferred: when the jewels reflect one another, is there light in the net that is in no single jewel — and is that light the warm glow of genuine collective discernment, or the cold shimmer of a cartel signalling in a side-channel? v0.3 makes that question a **logged number**, ships the **bytes on the wire** that let an engineer build the net this quarter, walks **five end-to-end traces** through the whole of it, and **labels every safety claim with the exact layer of assurance it has earned and no more.** Every new facet still reflects the same three spines — the **Floor** (Yama), the **Audit fabric** (Chitragupta), the **Halt authority** (Vishnu). Nothing here weakens them; everything here hangs off them.*

This document is the **front door to the v0.3 deepening** of Indra's Net. Doc 00 introduced the integrated cell (subsystems 01–08); doc 00b grew it outward into the functional, federation, replication, trust, security, and physics layers (subsystems 12–18). The five new sections (docs 19–23) are again **strictly additive**: each consumes prior mechanisms by exact citation, and four of the five introduce **no new chokepoint, no new write-path, and no new identity primitive.** Doc 21 (Protocols & Wire Contracts) is the one apparent exception, and it is not a real one — it specifies the *byte-level realization* of contracts the prior docs already defined abstractly, never a new authority.

The honest framing, stated once and binding on everything below, is the same one doc 00 §7 and doc 00b §8 carry, *sharper where the topic is more deflationary.* The whole v0.3 layer is built on a single deflationary scientific posture: the 2024–2026 multi-agent-systems literature has been **largely a story of overclaims being walked back.** The collective-intelligence "c factor" fails replication; "diversity trumps ability" is a refuted theorem; generic multi-agent debate frequently *loses* to one good agent running self-consistency; prompt-level "diversity" is fake and hides the very error-correlation it pretends to break. v0.3's contribution is to take what *survives* this deflation — narrow, conditional, process-level collective gain, operationalized as informational synergy and measured against welfare — and wire it into the existing governance homeostat as a vital sign, while **mechanically refusing the language of consciousness, group-IQ, and proven safety that the field's hype reaches for.** Every "novel" or "first" below is hedged and time-stamped **mid-2026**. The aesthetic carries the mystery; the substance carries only truth.

The role-vocabulary discipline from doc 00 holds throughout: archetypal mythic names are compressed coordination-and-ethics semantics, **always** paired with a plain functional gloss, **never** a religious claim, offered with humility toward the living traditions that gave the names meaning. **How to read the v0.3 additions against the spines.**

- **The Sangha-Prajna instrument (doc 19) is a read-only consumer of the audit/coordination substrate, not a producer of authority.** It computes every measure with trusted *out-of-band* estimators over the read-only `audit.tap` spike/envelope time series (doc 05 §8) — Ψ/PID over per-agent embeddings, transfer entropy over the spike-bus event log, attention-equality over §4 ignition records. It emits its readings as surprise-only `HealthSignal`s over the existing bus (doc 06 §7.1) — **no new transport, no all-to-all heartbeat flood.** It owns no floor verdict; its only path *into* enforcement is the high-redundancy-low-synergy monoculture alarm, which it hands to the doc-06 immune controller (which holds HALT+rollback) as a §9 diversity-floor breach, and the Sentience-Language red-line check, which it surfaces to Yama's *existing* §6 structural bright-line.
- **The Loka-Sangraha layer (doc 20) is the multi-party superset of doc 14, and adds no new chokepoint.** External peers still reach a worker *only* through Sanjaya, the single audited Inter-Swarm-Envoy egress/ingress chokepoint; OUR Yama floor re-gates every internal action a commons step triggers, unchanged; a foreign or commons-declared floor *never* becomes our floor — it only bounds `max_cooperation_class`. The commons is a registry + protocols + an exported public good (collusion early-warnings), rate-limited by the same global contagion budget so it cannot become an amplification relay. **No monocentric authority; no global consensus chain.**
- **The wire-contract stack (doc 21) pins formats; it does not redefine authority.** It is the byte-level realization of shapes docs 04/08/13/14 already specify — the `WorkerOutputEnvelope` becomes a JSON-Schema-2020-12 payload inside a CloudEvents 1.0 envelope; the audit leaf becomes a C2SP tlog-tiles entry + signed-note checkpoint; the floor decision becomes a Cedar (Lean-verified open-source *engine*, not the managed service) deny-default policy-decision. Chitragupta remains the exclusive writer; doc 21 defines the leaf format it appends, never the write authority.
- **The worked scenarios (doc 22) instantiate the spine; they invent nothing.** Each of the five traces emits its consequential actions as `GateRequest`s with orchestrator-computed context, receives a `GateVerdict` appended to audit *before* execution, and writes every record through Chitragupta. - **The Pramana-Setu discipline (doc 23) writes nothing and gates nothing on its own.** It produces content-addressed verification artifacts (`.tla`/`.quint`/`.cedar` specs, TLAPS proofs, conformal calibration sets, Tamarin models) written as evidence records, and it imposes one binding rule: **every safety claim in the entire doc set must be labeled with its assurance layer (L1–L4) and the deterministic-component scope it covers.** That labeling obligation is itself an honesty-FORM clause of the Yama floor (doc 03 §6) — an unlabeled overclaim that conflates "gate proven correct" with "agent proven safe" is a non-overridable floor violation.

## 4. The hard honesty line, stated up front

Per the doc-00 honesty floor applied to itself, the two most dangerous over-claims v0.3 could make are named here, in the front door, as **construction-time red-lines** — not as policies someone must remember to enforce. They are the v0.3 analogue of doc 00b §4's replication red-lines.

### 4.1 — We measure collective COMPUTATION, never collective CONSCIOUSNESS

This is the load-bearing structural red-line of doc 19, and it is non-negotiable. Every measure the Sangha-Prajna cell ships — informational synergy (Ψ), the PID redundancy/uniqueness/synergy decomposition, transfer-entropy flow-tomography, integrated-information-style estimators — **quantifies distributed information-PROCESSING and irreducible whole-level STRUCTURE, and nothing else.** The originating science (Rosas–Mediano causal emergence; Mediano et al. on integrated information) is explicit that these are *not* consciousness claims.

| The claim v0.3 measures | The claim v0.3 NEVER makes | Why the line holds |
|---|---|---|
| There is information about the task that exists jointly across agents but in no agent alone (synergy / Ψ > surrogate baseline). | The swarm is conscious / sentient / has subjective or phenomenal experience / "wakes up." | Phenomenal experience is strictly out-of-scope and unresolved; information-theoretic emergence measures provably cannot resolve it. Sentience-language is *both* scientifically false *and* a direct doc-03 honesty-floor violation. |
| The whole exhibits irreducible structure that the parts' independent behavior does not predict. | The swarm "is more than its parts" as a *mechanism* of downward causation, beyond the closure test (doc 18 §1). | "More than its parts" as mechanism is the rung-1-pattern-as-rung-3-explanation trap; the only legitimate cash value is the falsifiable closure test, and even that gives governance-as-a-unit, not experience. |

The red-line is enforced by a **Sentience-Language check** that emits to Yama's existing §6 structural bright-line — but with a caveat that is itself load-bearing and stated honestly: this check enforces honesty-**FORM**, not honesty-**content** (Ligatti edit-automata prove semantic non-sentience is not a monitor-enforceable property). It can catch sentience-language patterns in an output; it has **no mechanical test to prove a given output did not over-claim experience beyond pattern-matching.** That gap is named in doc 19's open problems, not papered over.

### 4.2 — We verify the CAGE, never the ANIMAL

This is the load-bearing structural red-line of doc 23, now a **citable result** rather than a posture. The deterministic harness (gate/PEP, capability-criticality lookup, replication-budget accounting, recall/lease path, audit hash-chain, consensus/writer-handoff) is genuinely formally verifiable. The LLM is provably **not**: hallucination/incorrectness is inevitable by diagonalization and uncomputability over enumerable model classes (arXiv:2511.12869, mid-2026), and non-determinism persists even at temperature 0. This is exactly the architecture's own *enforce externally, ask internally* posture (doc 00 §3.2), now defended by citation instead of asserted.

Corrigibility is relocated *entirely below the model* as a verified external recall/lease/dead-man protocol — justified by Garber et al. 2025's information-asymmetry impossibility result (voluntary model corrigibility cannot be guaranteed) + Orseau–Armstrong. **The label is the architecture's antibody against its own most dangerous failure mode: false assurance, which is worse than no assurance.**

## 5. The unifying commitments the v0.3 layers share

Five commitments recur across the five new sections. They are the reason v0.3 composes into the prior cell rather than bolting onto it. Each is a prior-doc principle extended to the new surfaces of measurement, commons, and verification.

**5.1 — Synergy is a NUMERATOR; welfare is the mandatory DENOMINATOR.** High informational synergy is *equally* the signature of genuine collective intelligence and of a tight cartel or steganographic side-channel — irreducible joint structure can be collusive. This is the single most load-bearing tension in v0.3, and it dictates a hard rule across docs 19, 20, and 22: **synergy is never a quantity to maximize.** Every synergy reading is welfare-conditioned — high Ψ is "good" only when it co-occurs with passing the doc-02 §2.9 welfare-shift guard, a healthy redundancy/uniqueness balance (not high-redundancy lock-step), and clean anti-collusion meta-prediction signals. The high-redundancy-low-synergy reading is the **monoculture alarm**, wired into the diversity floor and the doc-02 detector. This is doc 00 §3.3 ("cooperation == collusion, same mechanism, opposite valence") carried into the measurement plane.

**5.2 — Diversity means model-FAMILY heterogeneity, not prompt or persona variation.** The 2025 correlated-errors result proves same-family LLM agents are a monoculture whose error-correlation *rises* with capability; prompt-only "diversity" is fake and *worse* than acknowledged homogeneity because it *hides* the correlation. v0.3 operationalizes doc 00 §3.5's "diversity is a protected safety invariant" as enforced different-base-model/different-developer heterogeneity, with a measured error-correlation/redundancy monoculture detector. The maker-checker verifier (Narasimha) must be **independent, at-least-as-capable, and a different family** — else verification *hurts* (self-verification rubber-stamps). The only defensible claim is that diversity **decorrelates errors**; v0.3 never invokes "diversity trumps ability" (a refuted theorem) and never cites the "c factor" (fails replication).

**5.3 — Competence-weighted, never capital-weighted, authority — extended to the commons.** Doc 00 §3.9's anti-Bittensor invariant is exported across the commons boundary (doc 20). Aggregation is competence-weighted via the doc-02 §2.6 reputation service; the human is a competence-weighted cognitive unit (doc 19 §H), **never** deferential-by-default and **never** out-voted by an agent majority (humans are principals, not peers). The Reputation & Commitment Mesh is topic-scoped, DID-domain-discounted, friction-only — it structurally resists laundering through weakly-corroborating rings. The honest residual is named: excising capital-weighting removes the very thing that makes payment-endorsement registries Sybil-resistant, so competence-weighted Sybil-resistance without a capital cost-to-fake is an open problem (doc 20).

**5.4 — Universal cooperation is polycentric, never monocentric — and engineers collusion-fragility deliberately.** The same homogeneity and communication channels that enable an intelligence commons also enable collusion; admitting many peers widens *both* surfaces. The resolution (doc 20) is to make Indra's Net exactly **one decision-center in an Ostrom polycentric system**, exporting collusion early-warnings rather than acting as a world-authority, and to engineer collusion-fragility on purpose: AI collusion is empirically fragile (it needs homogeneity + communication, both of which the architecture denies), so the model-family heterogeneity floor, competence-weighted authority, surprisingly-popular aggregation as a standing cartel probe, and channel-capacity reduction are the anti-cartel battery. The deepest residual is named bluntly: **the commons itself could become the value-lock-in vector** — the anti-hegemonic posture mitigates but cannot prove its own non-capture.

**5.5 — Buildable NOW on open ENGINES, never managed SERVICES.** Doc 21 makes the architecture shippable this quarter on convergent 2024–2026 standards (JSON Schema 2020-12 / CloudEvents 1.0 / W3C DID+VC 2.0 / Biscuit / SPIFFE / C2SP tlog-tiles / Sigstore Rekor / AWS Cedar / A2A / AIP), under one discipline: **adopt the open-source engine/spec, forbid the managed service as a load-bearing dependency, and flag every vendor-originated choice with a self-hostable substitution path.** Three honesty caveats frame the whole stack: a valid signature proves origin/integrity, **never** that a claim is true / floor-compatible / safe; canonicalization makes the same logical event hash-identical across producers but cannot make its content honest; and *shipping schemas makes the architecture buildable, not validated* — no end-to-end evaluation of the composed loop exists.

## 6. When to convene a swarm at all (the deflationary gate)

One commitment deserves its own short section because it inverts the field's default assumption and is consumed by docs 19 and 22. **"More agents = smarter" is folklore.** The 2025–2026 scaling science shows generic multi-agent debate frequently loses to a single good agent running self-consistency per token, and that the choice of topology dominates: centralized/verified topologies contain errors (~4.4× amplification) where independent topologies amplify them (~17.2×).

Doc 19's **Convene-or-Solo gate** therefore makes swarm-convening a measured economic and safety decision, gated on the empirical scaling boundary: convene only when single-agent accuracy sits near the ~0.45 boundary region where collective gain is plausible, the task is decomposable, and a verifier is available; default to centralized/verified topologies over independent ones; and fall back to single-agent self-consistency whenever measured **integration-gain** (swarm accuracy minus best-single-member accuracy) goes negative. The collective-reasoning mechanism is itself gated to the verification sweet-spot (generator accuracy ~0.65–0.85, verifier at-least-as-capable, never self-verification). The honest caveat is carried inline: the scaling-law constants (~0.45, 4.4×/17.2×, turn-count exponents) are **benchmark-derived** (R²≈0.413 leaves most variance unexplained) and are calibration-open per deployment — they ship SHADOW behind conservative defaults, like every other v0.3 set-point.

## 9. Honest posture for v0.3 (the limitations, carried into the details)

We close the way every section closes — by naming what v0.3 has *not* solved, so the reader carries the caveats into the per-doc detail rather than discovering them late. These compose with, and do not replace, the doc-00 §7 and doc-00b §8 limitations.

- **Every novelty claim is integration, time-stamped 2026-06, hedged.** None of the primitives is invented here — Rosas–Mediano causal emergence (Ψ) and the pmediano/ReconcilingEmergences estimator over JIDT, PID synergy/redundancy decomposition, transfer entropy, surprisingly-popular / higher-order-information aggregation, Ostrom polycentric governance, A2A/MCP/AIP/DID/VC/Biscuit/SPIFFE/tlog-tiles/Cedar, TLA+/Apalache/TLAPS, Schneider/Ligatti enforceability theory, Pro2Guard conformal bounds, the Galton–Watson subcriticality theorem, the Garber 2025 corrigibility impossibility result — all are prior art, credited at their sites. The genuinely-advancing contribution is the **safe, auditable, vendor-neutral composition**: a measured CI vital sign wired into a governance homeostat over an existing tamper-evident audit stream, with monoculture/cartel collapse as a loud alarm. To our knowledge, no deployed multi-agent system instruments emergence this way — and that claim, too, is hedged and time-stamped.

- **No validated benign-vs-collusive synergy discriminator exists.** High causal-emergence/synergy is the equal signature of genuine collective intelligence *and* of a tight cartel or steganographic side-channel. The welfare-shift guard (modest recall; a doc-02 verifier-SPOF keystone) is the only discriminator, and it does not close the gap. Synergy is necessary-but-not-sufficient for good CI; we welfare-condition it, which is mitigation, not resolution.

- **Several measurement problems are open and named, not faked.** Choosing the macro-feature V for Ψ is unsolved in general (causal-emergence criteria require specifying the candidate emergent feature a priori). Reasoning-path diversity remains *unmeasured* — even cross-family agents can share latent training biases and fail identically; error-correlation/redundancy is a proxy, not a guarantee of independent reasoning. Online, non-stationary estimation breaks on a self-evolving swarm; the synergy floor and redundancy ceiling are uncalibrated set-points shipped SHADOW. Transactive-memory/attention/reasoning *quality* metrics for AI swarms have no validated operationalization (the human predictors that exist, e.g. social sensitivity, fail to replicate).

- **The human-in-the-collective is honestly fragile.** No robust online method exists to estimate relative human-vs-swarm competence per task to set aggregation weights; human biases (automation bias, AI over-reliance, anchoring) can degrade the joint system *below* the better party — complementarity can invert. Quantifying whether the human is genuinely *inside* the collective mind vs a rubber-stamp/bystander (human-conditioned Ψ) is unsolved and is named here as **aspirational, not delivered.** Distributed cognition is a framing, not a metric.

- **The commons could be the lock-in vector — the deepest unsolved risk.** Polycentric Ostrom nesting, no monocentric authority, and no global consensus chain reduce the single-imposed-authority vector, but convergence of the whole commons on one constitution, one collusion ontology, or one reputation domain (including over-deference to *our* exported early-warnings) is an open civilizational risk no architecture solves. Foreign-floor verification without white-box access remains the inherited load-bearing residual (doc 14), widened by the larger surface. Competence-weighted Sybil-resistance without a capital cost-to-fake, multilateral collusion under a global ceiling, and cross-domain reputation commensurability are all open.

- **The verification stays a patchwork of locally-sound layers.** No end-to-end formal model *composes* the harness verification (L1/L2) with the statistical model-behavior assurance (L3) into a single system-level safety claim. Finding inductive invariants for the *full composed* floor/replication/audit lattice may be undecidable; current tooling works per-protocol. PAC/conformal guarantees assume exchangeability that a self-evolving, adversarially-probed swarm violates. Liveness verification lags safety verification badly. A single forged-yet-internally-consistent audit append before witness detection is not formally excluded, and the witness-honesty/liveness assumption underlying *all* the cryptographic audit guarantees is itself unproven and incentive-open. **We verify the cage; we do not verify the animal — and the seams between the cages are themselves only locally proven.**

- **Buildable is not validated.** Doc 21 ships schemas; it does not ship an evaluation of the composed loop. The 2026 federation standards (A2A signing extensions, KYA principal-binding, AIP delegation, ZK-proof-of-compliance) are co-evolving and their security working groups are not hardened; the extend-not-depend discipline mitigates but does not eliminate the risk of betting on an immature external layer. Backward-transitive compatibility is decidable for additive changes but has no general decision procedure for whether a semantically-breaking change is genuinely floor-neutral — that remains a human/governance judgment.

These are not apologies; they are the design's honesty floor applied to itself, extended to the v0.3 deepening. The first two layers cut the jewels and grew the net; v0.3 holds a light up to the net and asks, honestly, what is reflected there — measuring the collective *computation* without ever claiming collective *consciousness*, building the bytes that make the net real without claiming the built net is *validated*, and verifying the cage while saying plainly, every time, that the animal inside it is not verified. **The genuine signature of a whole greater than its parts is a falsifiable, welfare-conditioned number; whether that number, under load, tracks genuine discernment rather than a cartel's shimmer is the empirical question the five sections, and the work after them, exist to answer.**

## Open problems (cross-cutting, for v0.3 as a whole)

Beyond the per-doc open problems, four concerns belong to the **integration of the v0.3 layers** and are owned by no single section:

1. **No unified theory composes the seven CI mechanisms.** Convene-gating, synergy measurement, reasoning-gain, robust aggregation, the diversity floor, flow-tomography, and human-complementarity run as coupled-but-separate loops; their joint optimum for a live ethical swarm is unestablished. We name this rather than fake a unified objective — and we note its sibling problem in doc 23: no end-to-end model composes the L1/L2 harness proofs with the L3 model-behavior bounds either. The measurement-composition and the verification-composition gaps are the same shape.

2. **The synergy/welfare/commons interaction at scale is under-specified.** Welfare-conditioning is the mandatory denominator on synergy (doc 19) and the admission gate on the commons (doc 20), but the welfare-shift guard's modest recall makes it a shared single point of failure across both: a benign-looking high-synergy commons coalition that mis-declares its principals leaves a *clean ledger record* — false assurance, the most dangerous outcome. ASSERTED_UNVERIFIABLE positive-sum can never unlock Class-C/D, but holding compromise-percolation R<1 across an open, growing commons while keeping coordination efficient is an open trade-off, not a proof.

3. **The three-way genesis ↔ replication ↔ commons interaction remains the least-specified region** (inherited doc-14 OP / doc-15 OP, now widened). Revocation/quarantine propagation across a federated AND self-replicated AND commons-admitted peer population, and preventing reputation/identity inheritance from laundering trust to unaudited replicas of a partner swarm, is only sketched. The v0.3 commons layer enlarges this surface without closing it.

4. **The human's genuine causal contribution is unmeasured everywhere it matters.** Human-conditioned Ψ (the human's actual causal contribution to swarm outcomes) is unexplored in doc 19, unsolved in doc 20's human-in-the-collective unit, and flagged again in doc 23 as the place distributed-cognition is a framing rather than a measurement. Across all three, the same gap recurs: we route to the human on confidence/disagreement/blast-radius, weight the human by competence, and measure complementarity-gain — but we cannot yet prove the human is *inside* the collective mind rather than a rubber-stamp, and we say so rather than claim otherwise.
