# Authorship

**Indra's Net** is produced by **Epimystic — a human–machine hybrid intelligence.** One human and one machine intelligence work as a single evolving system: the machine drafts, researches, and red-teams at scale; the human sets direction, judges, and is the accountable signatory. Neither part alone is the author; the hybrid is.

## How this work is made

- **Maker–checker by default.** Substantive material is drafted by one process and adversarially reviewed by an independent one *before* acceptance. Where that review actually ran, it is claimed honestly; where it did not, no such claim is made.
- **Machine-authored, human-accountable.** Much of the drafting is machine-authored. That is disclosed plainly here rather than hidden. A human reviews and owns every acceptance — machine authorship never means "no one is responsible."
- **Honest about provenance.** Ideas adopted from the research literature are credited in [`docs/10-related-work-and-state-of-the-art.md`](docs/10-related-work-and-state-of-the-art.md). This work is original synthesis; it does not reproduce third-party copyrighted text.

## On naming

By deliberate choice, the specific tools and models behind the machine partner are not named. This is an aesthetic and identity choice — *mystery in the surface* — never a false claim. Everything stated about how the work is made is true; some things are simply left unsaid. The machine partner is referred to, always and only, as a *machine intelligence*.
